New Tea Therapy

T E A L A B

New tea therapy features a soothing taste with a sweet aftertaste and refreshing aroma, replacing overly sugary hand-shaken drinks, chemical instant beverages, and even bitter herbal medicine.
By combining health-promoting herbs with high-quality floral teas, it caters to modern lifestyles, offering deliciousness, health benefits, tranquility, and convenience—quietly integrating wellness into daily life and creating a new culture of natural, additive-free healthy tea drinks.

Brand Story

The health of children is always a parent's greatest concern.

This is a truth that resonates deeply with Mr. Huang Qiming, the founder of "TeaLab."His daughter’s frail health from a young age filled him with worry, leading him to seek out renowned doctors for many years, yet to no avail.

After nearly a decade of effort, he finally found a distinguished physician who recommended Dendrobium as a key treatment.This helped to improve his daughter's constitution and boost her immunity, addressing her long-standing health issues.Having experienced the remarkable benefits of Dendrobium himself, Mr. Huang was inspired to promote its exceptional effects.Thus, he established "Hua Yan Cao Shuo," a tea therapy brand centered around Dendrobium, dedicated to bringing the best to everyone.

A Unique Wellness Experience

Tealab is a local original brand established in 2012, pioneering "Dendrobium New Tea Therapy" and leading traditional wellness tea towards a younger audience.

Using precious "Dendrobium flowers" paired with carefully selected floral ingredients from around the world, Tealab features stylish and unique packaging design along with thoughtful offerings. This allows everyone to enjoy the deliciousness of natural floral and fruit teas while addressing various health issues, gradually becoming a daily wellness habit and enhancing quality of life.
